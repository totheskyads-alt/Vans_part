Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Vans Parts Midlands ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website or contact us. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is protected in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who We Are

Vans Parts Midlands, 11A Showell Rd, Wolverhampton WV10 9LN, United Kingdom.

Contact: +44 7417 555201

What Data We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address (if provided)
  • Van details (make, model, year)
  • Enquiry details
  • IP address and browser data collected automatically via cookies

How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To provide quotes and services
  • To process parts orders
  • To improve our website
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data to third parties.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent — contact forms
  • Legitimate interests — running our business
  • Legal obligation — accounting, fraud prevention

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined above, and no longer than 3 years for enquiry data unless a longer period is required by law.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

To exercise any right, contact us at the address above.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to function correctly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies without your consent. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third Parties

We may share data with:

  • Google Maps (location services)
  • Email/hosting providers
  • Where required by law

All third parties are required to handle your data securely.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
