About Vans Parts Midlands

Van Specialists Serving
Customers Across the UK.

We built Vans Parts Midlands around a single focus: vans. Every part we stock, every repair we carry out, and every piece of advice we give is rooted in genuine expertise with commercial vans.

Our Story

Wolverhampton's Dedicated
Van Specialists

Vans Parts Midlands was founded with a clear purpose: to give van owners across the UK a specialist they can actually rely on. Not a general garage that fixes everything from city cars to HGVs, but a team with deep, focused knowledge of commercial vans.

We saw too many van owners getting the runaround — wrong parts ordered, poor diagnoses, repairs that didn't last, and prices that didn't feel fair. We set out to do it differently.

Based on Showell Road in Wolverhampton, we're accessible, local, and we care about every job we take on. Whether you need a part sourced, a complex engine repair, or your van recovered from the roadside — we're here.

What Makes Us Different

Our Approach to
Every Job

These aren't slogans — they're the standards we hold ourselves to every working day.

01

Vans Only — Total Focus

Our specialisation isn't a marketing line. It means better stock, faster diagnosis, and advice that comes from genuine hands-on experience with vans specifically — not adapted from car mechanics.

02

Honesty Before Everything

We tell customers the truth about their van's condition, the realistic options available, and what we'd recommend — even if that means suggesting a simpler or cheaper fix. Trust is more valuable to us than a bigger invoice.

03

Professional Quality, Every Time

Every repair and parts fitting is done to a proper standard. We don't cut corners. We want customers to come back — not to fix our mistakes, but because they know the job was done right first time.

04

Fair Pricing, Always

We price our work fairly. Parts are supplied at honest margins, labour is charged at a reasonable rate, and you'll always know the cost before we begin. No hidden charges, no pressure tactics.

05

We Respect Your Time

We know a van off the road means lost earnings. We respond quickly, work efficiently, and keep communication clear throughout — so you're never left guessing where things stand.
