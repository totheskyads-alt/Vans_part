Engine Repairs

From head gaskets and timing belts to turbo replacements and complete engine rebuilds. We handle complex engine work that other garages turn away.

Mechanical Repairs

Clutch replacements, gearbox work, brake servicing, wheel bearings, driveshafts, and all general mechanical repairs carried out by van-experienced mechanics.

Bodywork & Doors

Replacement doors, body panels, and van-specific bodywork. We source the right parts and fit them properly — no shortcuts, no bodge jobs.

Diagnostics & Electrical

Engine management lights, fault code reading, and electrical diagnosis. We use proper diagnostic equipment to find the real fault — not just reset the light.

DPF & Emissions Solutions

We diagnose, clean and replace Diesel Particulate Filters across all major van models. EGR valves, AdBlue systems and emissions faults also covered.

Suspension & Running Gear

We replace shock absorbers, springs, control arms, wheel bearings, CV joints and steering components — keeping your van safe and road-ready.

Gearbox & Clutch

Slipping clutch or gearbox problems? We repair and replace clutch kits, flywheels and manual or automatic gearboxes across all van makes.

Recovery & Breakdown

Broken down in or around Wolverhampton? We'll come to you, recover your van and get it back on the road — fast.
