Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Vans Parts Midlands uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described below.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function correctly and allow us to understand how visitors use our site.

Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function and cannot be disabled
  • Analytical cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with the site, used only with your consent
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and choices, used only with your consent

Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Maps which may set its own cookies when you interact with the map on our website. These are subject to Google's own privacy and cookie policy.

Your Choices

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies using the cookie banner when you first visit our website. You can also change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings.

How to Disable Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect how the website functions. Visit www.allaboutcookies.org for guidance on managing cookies in your browser.

More Information

For more information about how we handle your data, please read our Privacy Policy.
