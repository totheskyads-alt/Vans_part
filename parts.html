What We Supply

Quality Van Parts,
New & Used

At Vans Parts Midlands, we supply parts exclusively for vans. Whether you need a brand-new OEM component or a quality-checked used part to keep costs down, we can help.

We source parts for all the most popular van makes driven across the UK — from Ford Transits to VW Crafters, Vauxhall Vivaros to Mercedes Sprinters. If we don't have it on the shelf, we'll track it down.

All used parts are checked before sale. Our advice is always honest — if a part isn't the right solution, we'll tell you.

Parts Categories

What Parts Can We Help With?

From major mechanical components to body panels and electrical parts — we cover all the essentials for keeping your van on the road.

Engine Parts

Timing belts, head gaskets, oil pumps, pistons, cylinder heads, and engine ancillaries for all common van engines.

Doors & Body Panels

Front, side, and rear doors, bonnets, wings, bumpers, and body panels — new and used, matched to your van's make and colour.

Turbo & Fuel System

Turbochargers, intercoolers, EGR valves, fuel injectors, and fuel pumps. A common failure area we're very familiar with.

Lights & Electrical

Headlights, tail lights, indicators, alternators, starter motors, and van-specific electrical components sourced to spec.

Mechanical Components

Gearboxes, clutch kits, brake discs and pads, wheel bearings, driveshafts, CV joints, and steering components.

Suspension & Chassis

Shock absorbers, coil springs, wishbones, anti-roll bar links, and other suspension components to restore ride quality and safety.

Don't see what you need? Just ask — we can source most van parts on request.
